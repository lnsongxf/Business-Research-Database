The personnel effectiveness grid (PEG): A new tool for estimating personnel department effectiveness

Authors

  • Donald J. Petersen,

    Search for more papers by this author

    • Donald J. Petersen is Associate Professor of Management at Loyola University of Chicago, and a consultant to various business firms and the government in the areas of behavioral training, statistical and demographic studies, and personnel management. He is an arbitrator listed on the panels of the American Arbitration Association and the Federal Mediation Conciliation Service.

  • Robert L. Malone

    Search for more papers by this author

    • Robert L. Malone is Professor of Management and Chairman of the Department of Management of Loyola University of Chicago. He has been a manager, consultant, researcher and educator in personnel and business management for the past 20 years. He was earlier affiliated with United Air Lines, International Telephone and Telegraph Corporation, and Fry Consultants, Inc. Dr. Malone and Dr. Petersen are coauthors of a recently published book, The Effective Manager's Desk Book: Improving Results through People.
