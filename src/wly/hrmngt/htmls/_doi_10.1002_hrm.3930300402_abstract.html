Article

Total quality management and the human resource professional: Applying the baldrige framework to human resources

Authors

  • Christopher Hart,

    Search for more papers by this author

    • Christopher Hart, Ph.D., is founder and president of the TQM Group, a Boston-based consulting firm specializing in total quality management. He was formerly on the faculties of the Harvard Business School and Cornell University. Hart has serued as an examiner and trainer for the Malcolm Baldrige National Quality Awards program, has authored dozens of articles and books on quality, and focuses his work on helping companies design and implement quality-improvement programs.

Abstract

The still evolving discipline of total quality management (TQM) has left many humanresource professionals confused about their role. The authors believe that the HR function personnel should spearhead company quality efforts, as well as assess the performance of their own function, by using the Malcolm Baldrige National Quality Awards framework.
